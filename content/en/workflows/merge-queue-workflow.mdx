# Merge Queue Workflow

## Where this workflow fits

Merge queue becomes valuable when many pull requests are competing for the same protected branch and branch freshness keeps expiring between review and merge.

## What it solves

- repeated "base changed, re-run checks" cycles
- race conditions between multiple approved PRs
- human coordination overhead around who merges next

## Common mistakes

- assuming merge queue replaces review quality
- enabling queue before CI and branch rules are stable
- treating queue as a universal default for very small teams
