# Sync Boundaries on Shared Branches

## Why shared branches are different

Once multiple people depend on the same branch, actions that feel harmless on a personal branch can become destructive team-wide.

## A useful rule

Prioritize "everyone can sync safely" over "history looks maximally tidy."

## Safer boundaries

- allow fetch, ff-only pulls, explicit merges, and deliberate review merges
- treat shared-branch rebases with care
- avoid force-push and reset-based history rewrites unless the team explicitly coordinates them
